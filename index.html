About KUET

Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET) is dedicated to advancing knowledge, innovation, and technology. With a strong focus on research and excellence, KUET aims to shape the next generation of engineers, scientists, and leaders.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in engineering and technology education and research, contributing to the nation’s sustainable development.

Our Mission

Empowering students with cutting-edge knowledge and skills to address global challenges and innovate for a better tomorrow.
